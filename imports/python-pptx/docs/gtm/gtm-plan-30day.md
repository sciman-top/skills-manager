## Post-30-Day Decision Points

**If revenue > $5K and pipeline > $25K**: Continue current approach, hire part-time BD support, expand to Tier 2/3 contacts.

**If revenue > $5K but pipeline thin**: The expert network model works but direct sales needs refinement. Invest in conference attendance (OTC, Subsea Tieback) for face-to-face credibility.

**If revenue < $5K**: Reassess positioning. Consider: (a) packaging tools for license instead of consulting, (b) partnering with a larger firm as their parametric screening subcontractor, (c) targeting a different persona (project engineers instead of managers).
