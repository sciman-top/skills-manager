# Field Development Plan (FDP) Analysis References

## Key Textbooks
- **Faltinsen, O.M.** (1990). *Sea Loads on Ships and Offshore Structures*. Cambridge University Press.
- **Newman, J.N.** (1977). *Marine Hydrodynamics*. MIT Press.
- **Sarpkaya, T., & Isaacson, M.** (1981). *Mechanics of Wave Forces on Offshore Structures*. Van Nostrand Reinhold.
- **Chakrabarti, S.K.** (2005). *Handbook of Offshore Engineering*. Elsevier.
- **Gudmestad, O.T., & Moan, T.** (1996). *Fatigue and Strength Theory of Marine Structures*. Springer.

## Industry Standards
### DNV Standards
- **DNV-RP-C203**: Fatigue design of offshore steel structures
- **DNV-RP-C204**: Design against accidental loads
- **DNV-RP-C205**: Environmental conditions and environmental loads
- **DNV-OS-C101**: Design of offshore steel structures, general (LRFD method)
- **DNV-OS-C201**: Design of offshore steel structures, plasticity and fracture
- **DNV-OS-C202**: Offshore pipelines
- **DNV-OS-C301**: Position mooring
- **DNV-OS-E301**: Position monitoring and emergency response

### API Standards
- **API RP 2A-WSD**: Recommended Practice for Planning, Designing and Constructing Fixed Offshore Platforms (Working Stress Design)
- **API RP 2A-LRFD**: Recommended Practice for Planning, Designing and Constructing Fixed Offshore Platforms (Load and Resistance Factor Design)
- **API RP 2SK**: Design and Analysis of Stationkeeping Systems for Floating Structures
- **API RP 2MET**: Metocean Conditions and Environmental Loads
- **API RP 2RD**: Design and Analysis of Subsea Pipeline Systems
- **API RP 2SIM**: Subsea Pipeline Systems – Design, Installation and Operation
- **API RP 2T**: Criteria for Evaluating the Ability of Fixed Offshore Structures to Withstand Hurricane Forces
- **API RP 2U**: Planning, Designing, and Constructing Tension-Leg Platforms
- **API RP 2Z**: Development and Operation of Subsea Production Systems

### ISO Standards
- **ISO 19900 series**: Petroleum and natural gas industries – Specific requirements for offshore structures
  - ISO 19901-1: General requirements
  - ISO 19902: Fixed steel offshore structures
  - ISO 19903: Fixed concrete offshore structures
  - ISO 19904-1: Floating offshore structures - General requirements (based on tension-leg platforms)
  - ISO 19904-2: Floating offshore structures - Floating offshore platforms (other than tension-leg platforms)
  - ISO 19905-1: Site-specific assessment of mobile offshore foundations - Spudcan foundations
  - ISO 19906: Arctic offshore structures

## Key APIs and Data Sources
### Metocean Data
- **NOAA**: National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (tides, currents, winds, waves)
- **ECMWF**: European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ERA5 reanalysis)
- **Copernicus Marine Service**: Global ocean monitoring and forecasting
- **NOSHMO**: Norwegian Ocean Show Modelling System
- **NORA3**: Norwegian Reanalysis (10km hindcast for North Sea, Norwegian Sea, Barents Sea)

### Geotechnical Data
- **NGI**: Norwegian Geotechnical Institute (soil databases, CPT interpretations)
- **USGS**: United States Geological Survey (seismic data, sediment properties)
- **BGS**: British Geological Survey (seabed sediments, geohazards)
- **GEBCO**: General Bathymetric Chart of the Oceans (global bathymetry)

### Vessel and Traffic Data
- **MarineTraffic**: AIS data for vessel tracking
- **Spire Global**: Satellite-based AIS and weather data
- **ExactEarth**: AIS data services

### Environmental and Regulatory
- **OSPAR**: Convention for the Protection of the Marine Environment of the North-East Atlantic
- **IMO**: International Maritime Organization (ballast water, emissions, safety)
- **BOEM**: Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (US offshore data, lease maps)
- **BSEE**: Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (US offshore safety)
- **NPD**: Norwegian Petroleum Directorate (fact pages, maps, production data)
- **UK OGA**: UK Oil and Gas Authority (now NSTA) - data and licensing

### Software and Tools
- **Orcina OrcaFlex**: Dynamic analysis of marine systems
- **ANSYS AQWA**: Hydrodynamic analysis and design of marine structures
- **SESAM**: DNV's integrated software suite for offshore engineering
- **OpenFOAM**: Open-source CFD for wave-structure interaction
- **WAMIT**: Wave analysis by MIT (frequency-domain hydrodynamics)
- **HydroSTE**: Hydrodynamic analysis tool (time-domain)
